About the Dish

Origin: Ichigo-ni is a specialty of Aomori Prefecture, named for the sea urchins resembling strawberries ("ichigo") in the broth.
Flavor: A light dashi broth with soy sauce and sake, highlighting the natural sweetness of sea urchins and abalone.
Cultural Significance: Served during festive occasions, Ichigo-ni represents Aomori’s coastal bounty and refined regional cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 100g fresh sea urchin
  • 100g abalone (or scallops, sliced thin)
  • 3 cups kombu dashi broth
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp soy sauce

Instructions

  1. Baked Chicken and Veggies: Marinate chicken with olive oil, garlic, salt, paprika. Cut veggies, toss with oil, salt, herbs. Bake at 400°F for 25-30 mins. Garnish with herbs.
  2. Simple Pasta: Boil pasta. Sauté garlic, add tomatoes, spinach, cream or cheese. Toss with pasta. Top with Parmesan and basil.
  3. Dessert Mug Cake: Mix flour, sugar, cocoa in a mug. Add milk, oil, vanilla, stir. Microwave 1-2 mins. Top with ice cream or syrup.
