About the Dish

Origin: Hittsumi Jiru is a traditional dish from Iwate Prefecture, created as a hearty meal for rural communities.
Flavor: A savory soup with thick wheat flour dumplings, vegetables, and meat, often flavored with soy sauce and miso.
Cultural Significance: A comforting, rustic dish tied to the region’s farming heritage, enjoyed during colder months.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 150g chicken thigh, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 burdock root, julienned
  • 1/2 carrot, sliced
  • 1/2 daikon radish, sliced
  • 3 shiitake mushrooms, sliced
  • 4 cups dashi broth
  • 2 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 Tbsp sake
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Easy Chicken Stir-Fry: Dice chicken, chop veggies, sauté chicken, add veggies, cook, mix sauce, stir, garnish, serve.
  2. Cheesy Garlic Bread: Make garlic butter, spread on bread, top with cheese, bake, sprinkle parsley.
  3. Chocolate Mug Cake: Mix dry ingredients, add wet, stir, microwave, top with cream or chips.
