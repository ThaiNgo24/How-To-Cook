About the Dish

Origin: Coastal Hokkaido.
Flavor: Sweet, succulent crab infused into seasoned rice.
Cultural Significance: Highlights Hokkaido's status as a premium seafood producer.

Ingredients

  • Short-grain cooked rice (Japanese rice)
  • Crab meat (fresh or cooked, shredded)
  • Boiled egg (chopped or made into tamago, Japanese rolled omelet)
  • Soy sauce
  • Mirin (sweet Japanese rice wine)
  • Sake (Japanese rice wine)
  • Sugar
  • Dashi (optional, fish stock)
  • Vegetables (e.g., chopped green onions, grated carrots, sweet corn, peas)

Instructions

  1. Cook short-grain rice according to package instructions.
  2. Shred the crab meat (either raw or cooked).
  3. Boil the egg, chop it, or prepare a tamago (rolled omelet).
  4. In a small bowl, mix soy sauce, mirin, sake, sugar, and optional dashi for flavor.
  5. Chop and lightly cook vegetables like green onions, carrots, corn, and peas if needed.
  6. Mix the cooked rice with the crab meat, egg, and vegetables. Add the seasoning mixture and stir well.
  7. Serve the Kani Meshi warm, garnished with extra vegetables or seasonings as desired.
