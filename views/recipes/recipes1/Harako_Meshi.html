About the Dish

Origin: Harako Meshi is a specialty of Miyagi Prefecture, originating from its coastal fishing communities.
Flavor: A combination of tender salmon, salmon roe, and rice steamed with soy sauce and dashi, offering a rich umami flavor.
Cultural Significance: Celebrating the region’s fishing culture and seasonal salmon abundance, Harako Meshi is a beloved autumn dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 150g salmon fillet, grilled and flaked
  • 1/4 cup salmon roe (ikura)
  • 2 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 Tbsp mirin
  • 1 tsp sake
  • 1/2 sheet nori, shredded

Instructions

  1. One-Pot Pasta: Cook pasta, veggies, protein, and broth in one pot until tender, garnish with cheese and herbs.
  2. Quick Snacks: Cheesy Nachos: Bake tortilla chips with cheese, beans, veggies, serve with salsa. Energy Balls: Mix oats, nut butter, honey, add-ins, shape into balls, chill.
  3. Dessert Hacks: Ice Cream Sandwich: Place ice cream between two cookies. Chocolate Bark: Spread melted chocolate on parchment, top with nuts or fruit, chill.
