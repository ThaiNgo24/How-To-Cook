About the Dish

Origin: A traditional dish from Akita Prefecture, made from mashed rice grilled on skewers and added to a hot pot.
Flavor: Soy sauce-based broth with chicken, burdock root, mushrooms, and vegetables. The grilled rice sticks absorb the broth, offering a unique texture.
Cultural Significance: Enjoyed in autumn and winter, symbolizing warmth and family gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 6-8 chicken wings or drumettes
  • 1/2 burdock root, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 onion, sliced
  • 1 carrot, thinly sliced
  • 4 shiitake mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/2 bunch Japanese parsley (mitsuba), chopped
  • 4 cups dashi broth
  • 2 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 Tbsp sake
  • 1 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Mini Garlic Monkey Bread: Coat dough with butter, garlic, and herbs. Bake at 350°F for 12-15 minutes.
  2. Pistachio Jello Salad: Mix pudding, whipped topping, pineapple, and marshmallows. Chill for 2 hours.
  3. Asian Stir-Fry: Stir-fry protein and veggies, coat with soy sauce, garlic, ginger, and honey. Serve with rice/noodles.
  4. Creamy Soup: Sauté veggies, add stock and cream, blend if desired. Serve with croutons/cheese.
  5. Roasted Veggies: Toss with olive oil, garlic, and herbs. Roast at 425°F for 20-25 minutes.
