About the Dish

Origin: Senbei Jiru comes from Hachinohe, Aomori Prefecture, where rice crackers (senbei) are used in soup.
Flavor: The soy sauce-based broth, with chicken, mushrooms, and vegetables, is enriched by the chewy texture of softened rice crackers.
Cultural Significance: Aomori’s comfort food, showcasing the region’s resourcefulness in turning simple ingredients into a hearty meal.

Ingredients

  • 6-8 pieces rice crackers (senbei)
  • 150g chicken thigh or pork, sliced
  • 1/2 carrot, sliced
  • 1/2 daikon radish, sliced
  • 1/2 onion, sliced
  • 3 shiitake mushrooms, sliced
  • 4 cups dashi broth
  • 2 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 Tbsp sake
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Pan-Seared Chicken with Veggies: Season chicken with olive oil, salt, pepper, spices. Sear in skillet until golden. Add sliced veggies, season with garlic and herbs. Serve together.
  2. Cheesy Stuffed Bread: Flatten dough, stuff with cheese, seal. Bake or fry until golden. Brush with butter, sprinkle herbs.
  3. Quick Chocolate Dessert: Mix melted chocolate, crushed biscuits, condensed milk. Shape, chill until firm. Dust with cocoa or drizzle syrup.
