Ingredients

  • 250g prawn meat
  • 1/2 tbsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1/2 tsp chicken granules
  • 1 tsp caster sugar
  • 150g tapioca starch

Instructions

  1. Finely chop or mince the prawn meat.
  2. Mix the prawn meat with salt, pepper, chicken granules, and caster sugar.
  3. Add tapioca starch to the mixture and knead until a dough forms.
  4. Divide the dough into small balls, then flatten each ball into a thin patty.
  5. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and cook the patties until both sides are golden brown and crispy.
  6. Allow the Ebi Senbei to cool before serving.
